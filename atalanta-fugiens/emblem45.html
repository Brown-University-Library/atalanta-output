Epigramme Epigram 45.


The Sunne Sun noe no bodyes bodies dense can penetrate,
And soe so the adverse parts a shade create:
Which, though it be of all things least sett set by,
Is much conducing to Astronomy;
But more to th' the Sophi are the Sunne Sun and shade,
Because they perfect the auriferous trade.
