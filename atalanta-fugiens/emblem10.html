Epigramme Epigram 10.


The worlds whole frame depends upon this chaine chain,
Like to its like, as Shepheardesse Shepherdess to Swaine Swain:
These are the bounds of art, proceed noe no higher,
Hermes to Hermes joyne join, and fire to fire.
Vulcan makes Hermes fly, but Cynthia must
Bring him to tears, then yeild yield to her brothers lust.
