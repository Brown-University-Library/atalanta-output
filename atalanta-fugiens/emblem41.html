Epigramme Epigram 4i.


Myrrha, conspiring with her Sire, gave breath
To th' the fair Adonis, tushd tushed by a Boar to death:
Fond Venus runns runs, with thorne thorn in foot, which bled,
And therewith tingd tinged the lilly Roses red;
The Goddesse Goddess weeps, the Syrians can't refraine refrain,
Hee's He's layd laid amongst the lettice lettuce being Slayne Slain
