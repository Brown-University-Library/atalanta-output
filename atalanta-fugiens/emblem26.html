Epigramme Epigram 26.


To greater wisedome wisdom man cannot attaine attain,
Than health with wealth to acquire, if not in vaine vain:
Shee She in her right hand length of dayes days extends,
Her left vast heaps of treasure comprehends;
To him that shall espouse her as a wife
Shee She (as the tree) will yeild yield the fruit of life.
