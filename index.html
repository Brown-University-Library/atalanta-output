Furnace and Fugue brings to life in digital form an interdisciplinary seventeenth-century text, Michael Maier’s lavishly illustrated musical emblem book. Atalanta fugiens, of 1618, reinterprets Ovid’s myth of Atalanta as an alchemical allegory via a series of fifty emblems, each of which contains text, image, and a fugue for three voices. Re-rendering Maier’s multimedia masterpiece as an online publication, Furnace and Fugue allows readers to hear, see, manipulate, and investigate Atalanta fugiens in ways that were perhaps imagined when it was composed but were simply impossible to realize in full before now.
