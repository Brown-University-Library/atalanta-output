With Furnace and Fugue, our objective is to digitally enhance the 10 scholarly essays that form the interpretative component of the project. The publication will also include a digital edition of Atalanta fugiens, a 17th-century alchemical emblem book by Michael Maier; and newly commissioned vocal recordings of 50 fugues.
